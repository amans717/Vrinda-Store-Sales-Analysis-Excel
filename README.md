# 📊 Vrinda Store Data Analysis Dashboard (MS Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing **Vrinda Store's 2022 sales data** using **Microsoft Excel** to generate actionable business insights through an interactive dashboard. The dashboard enables the business owner to understand customer behavior, sales performance, and market trends, helping drive data-driven decisions for improving sales in 2023 and beyond.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze the annual sales data of **Vrinda Store** and build an interactive dashboard that helps answer key business questions and supports strategic decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Data Cleaning
- Data Validation
- Interactive Dashboard

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset contains sales transactions for the year **2022**, including customer demographics, product categories, order status, sales channels, and state-wise sales information.

---

## 📈 Key Business Questions (KPIs)

- Compare monthly **Sales vs Orders** using a single chart.
- Identify the month with the **highest sales and orders**.
- Determine whether **Men or Women** purchased more products.
- Analyze the **order status distribution**.
- Find the **Top 10 states** contributing to total sales.
- Analyze the relationship between **Age Group and Gender** based on the number of orders.
- Identify the **highest contributing sales channel**.
- Determine the **highest-selling product category**.
- Calculate the **percentage of successfully delivered orders**.

---

## ⚙️ Project Workflow

1. Collected and explored the dataset.
2. Cleaned the data by removing inconsistencies, missing values, and duplicate records.
3. Standardized data formats and validated data quality.
4. Created Pivot Tables for each business requirement.
5. Designed Pivot Charts to visualize key insights.
6. Integrated all visualizations into a single interactive dashboard.
7. Added **Slicers** for dynamic filtering and easy data exploration.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 👩 **Women customers** contributed approximately **65%** of total purchases.
- 📍 **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** were the top three revenue-generating states.
- 👥 Customers aged **30–49 years** placed around **50%** of all orders.
- 🛒 **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra** were the highest-performing sales channels.
- 📦 More than **90%** of all orders were successfully delivered.
- 👗 Clothing was the highest-selling product category.

---

## ✅ Business Recommendation

Based on the analysis, Vrinda Store should focus its marketing efforts on **women aged 30–49 years** living in **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh**. Personalized promotions, targeted advertisements, and platform-specific campaigns on **Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra** can significantly improve customer engagement and increase future sales.
